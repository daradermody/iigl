The IIGL (think iggle) is an Irish video gaming league where companies compete against each other for the prestige of... a name on a wall. And charities. That's important too. If you work for a company, and think you're the bees knees, submit a team!

The IIGL is the eSports equivalent of inter-firms GAA or any other inter-firms sport. And just like other inter-firms sports leagues, the IIGL is run voluntarily by passionate individuals who work regular day jobs. Our goal is simple: to promote gaming! We use Battlefy as the tournament platform because it's free, automated and awesome!

In the IIGL, we've substituted the traditional wooden shield with our walls of fame. Walls plural? Yup, we have a wall for every game! Also, since we're just getting started we're looking at YOU to become part of the foundations!
