IIGL

(Irish Inter-firms Gaming League)

Like inter-company GAA, but with 16GB RAM

The IIGL (think iggle) is an Irish video gaming league where companies compete against each other for the prestige of... a name on a wall.

And charities. That's important too.

The IIGL is the eSports equivalent of inter-firms GAA or any other inter-firms sport; and just like other inter-firms sports leagues, the IIGL is run voluntarily by passionate individuals who work regular day jobs. The goal of the IIGL is fairly simple: to promote gaming. We use Battlefy as the tournament platform because it's free, automated and awesome!

If you work for a company, and think you're the bees knees at online video games, round up some people together and submit a team. Why? Same reason anyone does anything competitively... to win! Here at the IIGL we've substituted the traditional wooden shield with our walls of fame. Walls plural? Yup, we have a wall for every game and there's nothing stopping you getting your name on all of them! Save for maybe the competition! Also, since we're just getting started we're looking at YOU to become part of the foundations!
